About This Collection

These educational infographics were developed as part of a comprehensive laboratory training and competency program. Each infographic is designed to:

  • Enhance Visual Learning: Complex procedures simplified into clear, step-by-step visual guides
  • Support Competency Assessment: Align with CAP/CLIA training requirements
  • Standardize Procedures: Ensure consistency across all shifts and staff
  • Reduce Errors: Quick reference guides at point-of-care to minimize mistakes

Note: These are sample infographics with organizational branding removed. The full collection includes 130+ infographics covering all laboratory departments.
